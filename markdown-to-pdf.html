What is LuxPDF?

LuxPDF is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, LuxPDF offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our Markdown to PDF tool?

  • Completely Free & Unlimited: Convert as many Markdown files to PDF as you need, without any cost or restrictions.
  • Privacy First: Your files are processed directly in your browser. They are never uploaded to our servers, guaranteeing 100% privacy.
  • Open Source: Our code is publicly available, so you can verify our privacy claims and even contribute to the project.
  • Preserves Structure: Basic structure such as headings, lists, paragraphs, and line breaks is preserved.
  • No Registration Required: Use our tools instantly without the need to create an account or provide any personal information.
  • Batch Conversion: Convert multiple Markdown files into separate PDFs in a single operation.

How to use the Markdown to PDF tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your .md or .markdown files onto the upload area, or click to select them from your device.
  2. Step 2: Click the "Process Files" button to start the conversion.
  3. Step 3: Once processed, download your new PDF files from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What Markdown features are supported?

This tool focuses on text-oriented rendering. It preserves headings, lists, paragraphs, blockquotes, code blocks, and inline code. Complex layouts and embedded images are not rendered.

Can I convert multiple Markdown files into a single PDF?

This tool converts each Markdown file into its own separate PDF document. If you wish to combine them, you can use our 'Merge PDF' tool after the conversion.

Is it safe to convert my Markdown files here?

Yes. The entire conversion process happens in your browser. Your files are never sent to or stored on our servers, ensuring your data remains completely private and secure.
