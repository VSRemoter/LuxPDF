Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is HEIF format?

HEIF (High Efficiency Image Format) is Apple's modern image format that provides better compression than JPEG while maintaining higher quality. It's the underlying format used in HEIC containers and is the default format for photos taken on newer iPhones and iPads.

Can I convert multiple HEIF files into a single PDF?

Yes! You can choose to either convert each HEIF file into its own separate PDF document, or combine all images into a single PDF with each image on its own page.

Will the image quality be preserved?

Yes, our tool maintains the original image quality during conversion. The HEIF images are converted to high-quality format before being embedded in the PDF.

Is it safe to convert my personal photos here?

Absolutely. The entire conversion process happens in your browser. Your photos are never sent to or stored on our servers, ensuring your personal images remain completely private and secure.
