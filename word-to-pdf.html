What is LuxPDF?

LuxPDF is an open-source, privacy-focused, and completely free suite of online PDF tools. We believe that your documents are your business, and we are committed to providing powerful, reliable tools without ever collecting your file data. Our platform runs entirely in your browser, ensuring that your files never leave your computer. With no ads, no registration, and no usage limits, LuxPDF offers a seamless and secure experience for all your PDF needs.

Why use our DOCX to PDF tool?

  • High Quality: Uses Mammoth.js to faithfully interpret Word content, then renders to PDF with html2canvas + jsPDF.
  • 100% Private: Runs entirely in your browser. Files never leave your device.
  • Free & Open Source: No registration, ads, or limits.
  • Batch Conversion: Convert multiple DOCX files in one go.

How to use the Word to PDF tool:

  1. Step 1: Drag and drop your .docx files into the upload area, or click to select them.
  2. Step 2: Click the "Process Files" button to start the conversion.
  3. Step 3: Once processed, download your new PDF(s) from the results section.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Are my Word files uploaded to a server?

No. Everything happens in your browser. We never upload or collect your files.

Will the formatting be preserved?

We use Mammoth.js for DOCX parsing and high-quality rendering. Most text and layout are preserved well, though very complex documents may need minor adjustments.

Can I convert multiple DOCX files at once?

Yes, batch conversion is supported. Add multiple files before processing.
